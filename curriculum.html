The Journey

Here at Creative Kidz Kinder Care we believe in the words caring, friendly, exploratory, loving, safe, and colorful. When your child leaves this daycare they will learn each and every one of those words. Below you will find that we support all age groups. For example, infants, toddlers, two year olds, preschool, 3 year olds, Jr, Kindergarten, and School Age Programs.

Infants

Ages: 6wks to 16 months

“Primary Care” is the key phrase in understanding the quality of care your baby will receive as a part of our Infant Care Program.

Toddlers

Two Year Olds

Ages: 24-36 months

Our Two Year Old Room is set up for what in some cases can be the most difficult time in a child’s life; the year in which a child may be expected to start caring for his/her self: using the bathroom, wiping, washing their hands, dressing themselves, and proper eating etiquette.

More Information

Preschool

Ages: 4 year olds

At this age our children’s brains are like sponges. Our teachers’ goal each day is to educate and expose our students to the world around them.
